// FAQ
What is TRASHCORE?
TRASHCORE is a fast, anonymous media hosting service. Upload images, videos, and audio — get a permanent public link instantly. No account required.
How large can my files be?
Each file can be up to 50MB. You can upload up to 10 files in one batch. Supported types: images, videos, and audio.
How do I delete my file?
After uploading, you receive a delete token. Save it — it is the only way to delete your file. Use the "Delete a File" tool in the menu and enter your token.
Are uploaded files private?
No. All uploaded files are publicly accessible via their URL. Anyone with the link can view the file. Do not upload private or sensitive content.
How long are files stored?
Files may be stored indefinitely, but we reserve the right to remove files at any time without notice — especially those violating our terms.
Is my IP address logged?
Yes. Your IP is logged for abuse prevention purposes. This data is not sold or shared with third parties except when legally required.
What file types are supported?
Images (jpg, png, gif, webp, etc.), Videos (mp4, webm, mov, etc.), and Audio (mp3, wav, ogg, etc.). Other file types will be rejected.
How do I report abuse?
Contact us via WhatsApp (+254 787 909 276) or Telegram (@trashcoredev2) with the file URL and reason. We respond as fast as possible.

Your Rights
